Tokenomics

Supply

The total supply of CUM is capped at 690,420,000. This is the maximum amount of CumCoins that will exist.

Distribution

A pool of 30,000,000 CUM is allocated for the creators and initial funders of the coin. An additional 200,000,000 CUM will be distributed through a decentralized crowdsale. The remaining coins will be distributed through UniSwap. UniSwap liquidity will be determined by Ethereum raised in the crowdsale.

Upgradeability

Using OpenZeppelin upgradeable ERC20 contracts, CumCoin can be upgraded in the case an exploit is found or the community has agreed on some changes. None of these upgrades will be decided on and added to the blockchain until a big majority of the community has agreed on them, and all proposed changes will be published to the official Github repository first.

Join The Movement

Before contributing, read this. While we all believe in CumCoin and want it to be successful, you need to be aware of the risks involved before making any contributions. That's why we want to make this clear: don't contribute more than you're prepared to lose. When you contribute to CumCoin, you're not buying CUM from anyone. Instead you're interacting with a decentralized contract on the Ethereum blockchain. By calling the contract's buyTokens function, you are set a balance internally in the contract, which you can check at any time using the balanceOf function. Note, you will not receive any tokens at this point. Once the crowdsale is over, you'll be able to call the withdrawTokens function. Calling this function will initiate a transfer of CUM from the crowdsale contract to the given address. Your entire balance will be transferred to the given address, after which your balance in the crowdsale will be reset to 0, preventing further withdrawals. By contributing to CumCoin, you understand that all contributions are final, and there cannot be any refunds or cancellations. You are also aware that there is no business behind CumCoin - you are simply interacting with a decentralized contract on the Ethereum blockchain. You understand that all decisions you make regarding CumCoin are your own, and you will bear their consequences. Again, don't contribute more than you're prepared to lose.
